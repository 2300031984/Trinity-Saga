Synopsis

Aquaman forges an uneasy alliance with an unlikely ally to save Atlantis and the world from irreversible destruction.

Plot

Arthur Curry, now King of Atlantis, faces a new threat as Black Manta seeks revenge and unleashes an ancient power. Arthur must unite old and new allies to protect his kingdom and the surface world, confronting his own legacy and the responsibilities of leadership.
