Synopsis

The Shazam family must face the Daughters of Atlas, who seek revenge and threaten to destroy the world with their ancient powers.

Plot

Billy Batson and his foster siblings, all with powers of Shazam, struggle with their new responsibilities. When the Daughters of Atlas arrive, seeking to reclaim their magic and unleash chaos, the family must unite to save the world. The film explores themes of family, heroism, and sacrifice, with epic battles and heartfelt moments.
