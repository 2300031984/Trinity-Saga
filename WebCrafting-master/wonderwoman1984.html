Synopsis

Diana Prince comes into conflict with the Soviet Union during the Cold War in the 1980s and finds a formidable foe in the form of the Cheetah and the mysterious Maxwell Lord.

Plot

In 1984, Diana Prince works at the Smithsonian and secretly fights crime as Wonder Woman. When a mysterious artifact grants wishes with dangerous consequences, Diana must face the ambitious businessman Maxwell Lord and her former friend Barbara Minerva, who transforms into the Cheetah. Diana is forced to make heartbreaking sacrifices to save the world from chaos and restore hope.
