Synopsis

Nearly 5,000 years after he was bestowed with the almighty powers of the Egyptian gods—and imprisoned just as quickly—Black Adam is freed from his earthly tomb, ready to unleash his unique form of justice on the modern world.

Plot

Teth-Adam, an ancient champion with godlike powers, is awakened in the modern world. As Black Adam, he becomes both a hero and a threat, clashing with the Justice Society of America—Hawkman, Doctor Fate, Atom Smasher, and Cyclone. The film explores themes of power, justice, and redemption as Black Adam must choose between vengeance and heroism.
