Synopsis

Supervillains Harley Quinn, Bloodsport, Peacemaker, and a collection of cons at Belle Reve prison join the super-secret, super-shady Task Force X as they are dropped off at the remote, enemy-infused island of Corto Maltese.

Plot

Amanda Waller assembles a new Task Force X for a mission to destroy a Nazi-era laboratory and its secret experiment, Project Starfish. The team, including Harley Quinn, Bloodsport, Peacemaker, and King Shark, must navigate betrayal, chaos, and a giant alien starfish to complete their mission. The film blends dark humor, action, and heart as the squad faces impossible odds.
