A New Hope

The plot follows a galactic conflict where Rebel Alliance spies manage to steal plans for the Death Star, a massive, planet-destroying weapon built by the oppressive Galactic Empire. Princess Leia, a Rebel leader, has the plans but is captured by Darth Vader. However, her droids, R2-D2 and C-3PO, escape with the plans, landing on the desert planet Tatooine.On Tatooine, the droids are found by young Luke Skywalker, who discovers a message from Leia seeking help from Obi-Wan Kenobi, an old Jedi Knight. When Luke’s family is killed by Imperial troops searching for the droids, he joins Obi-Wan to fight the Empire. They recruit Han Solo and Chewbacca, smugglers with a ship called the Millennium Falcon, and head to deliver the plans to Leia’s father.The group arrives at the Death Star after it destroys Leia’s home planet, Alderaan. Obi-Wan disables the tractor beam so they can escape, sacrificing himself in a duel with Vader. With the plans analyzed, the Rebels find a weakness in the Death Star. Luke joins the Rebellion's X-wing squadron to destroy it, and, with guidance from Obi-Wan’s spirit and help from Han, successfully blows up the Death Star just before it can fire on the Rebel base. Leia honors Luke and Han for their bravery in a celebratory ceremony.
