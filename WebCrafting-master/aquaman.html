Synopsis

\n

\n Arthur Curry, the human-born heir to the underwater kingdom of Atlantis, must step forward to lead his people and be a hero to the world.\n

\n

Plot

\n

\n Arthur Curry, raised on the surface, discovers his royal Atlantean heritage and is drawn into a conflict between the surface world and Atlantis. With the help of Mera and Vulko, Arthur must retrieve the legendary Trident of Atlan and challenge his half-brother Orm for the throne. The film explores themes of identity, leadership, and unity, culminating in an epic underwater battle.\n
