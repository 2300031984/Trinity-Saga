The Empire Strikes Back

In this second installment, the Empire seeks revenge against the Rebel Alliance following the destruction of the Death Star. The rebels are forced to evacuate their base on the ice planet Hoth after a brutal assault led by Darth Vader. Luke Skywalker journeys to the swampy planet Dagobah to train with Jedi Master Yoda, while Han Solo, Princess Leia, Chewbacca, and C-3PO seek refuge but are captured by the bounty hunter Boba Fett, who is hired by the Empire.
