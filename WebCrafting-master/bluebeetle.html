Synopsis

Jaime Reyes, a recent college graduate, gains superpowers when an alien scarab grafts itself to his body, turning him into the superhero Blue Beetle.

Plot

Jaime Reyes discovers the alien scarab, which bonds to him and gives him a powerful exosuit. As Blue Beetle, Jaime must protect his family and community from those who seek to exploit the scarab's power. The film explores themes of family, responsibility, and identity as Jaime embraces his destiny as a hero.
