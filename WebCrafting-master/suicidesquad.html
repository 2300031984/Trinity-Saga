Synopsis

A secret government agency recruits imprisoned supervillains to form a defensive task force. Their first mission: save the world from a powerful, enigmatic threat, but the team's volatile nature may be their undoing.

Plot

Following Superman's death, Amanda Waller assembles Task Force X, a group of dangerous criminals including Deadshot, Harley Quinn, and Captain Boomerang, to combat metahuman threats. Their mission is to stop the Enchantress, an ancient witch possessing one of their own. The team must overcome their differences and personal demons to save Midway City, discovering unexpected heroism along the way.
