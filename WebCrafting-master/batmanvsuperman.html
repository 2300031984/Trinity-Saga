Synopsis

Fearing the unchecked power of Superman, Gotham City's own formidable, forceful vigilante, Batman, takes on Metropolis's most revered, modern-day savior. As the world wrestles with what sort of hero it really needs, a new threat arises, putting mankind in greater danger than ever before.

Plot

After the destructive battle in Metropolis, Bruce Wayne becomes convinced that Superman is a threat to humanity. As Batman, he sets out to stop Superman, while Lex Luthor manipulates both heroes for his own ends. The two heroes clash, but must ultimately unite to face the monstrous Doomsday, created by Luthor. The film explores themes of power, responsibility, and sacrifice, culminating in a battle that changes the world forever.
