Synopsis

\n

\n We all have a superhero inside us, it just takes a bit of magic to bring it out. In Billy Batson's case, by shouting out one word—SHAZAM!—this streetwise 14-year-old foster kid can turn into the adult superhero Shazam.\n

\n

Plot

\n

\n Billy Batson, a foster child searching for his mother, is chosen by the ancient wizard Shazam to be his new champion. By uttering \"Shazam!\", Billy transforms into an adult superhero with incredible powers. With the help of his foster brother Freddy, Billy learns to use his abilities and must face the villainous Dr. Sivana, who seeks Shazam's power for himself. The film blends humor, heart, and heroics as Billy discovers the true meaning of family and heroism.\n
