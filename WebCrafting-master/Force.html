The Force Awakens

In *The Force Awakens*, the galaxy faces a new threat from the sinister First Order, led by Supreme Leader Snoke and his enforcer, Kylo Ren. The Resistance, led by General Leia Organa, seeks Luke Skywalker, the last Jedi, who has vanished. Scavenger Rey, ex-stormtrooper Finn, and ace pilot Poe Dameron join forces to bring hope to the galaxy once more. Rey discovers her latent Force abilities, while the battle against the First Order reaches a critical point. In a climactic showdown, Rey confronts Kylo Ren, beginning her journey to becoming a Jedi and rekindling the battle for balance in the Force.
