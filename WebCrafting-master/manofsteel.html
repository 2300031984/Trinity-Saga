Synopsis

Clark Kent, a young man with extraordinary powers, learns that he is not of this Earth. As he discovers his true origins and embraces his destiny as Superman, he must become the symbol of hope for humanity and face the threat of General Zod, a fellow Kryptonian with his own vision for Earth.

Plot

The film follows Kal-El, sent to Earth from the dying planet Krypton and raised as Clark Kent by human parents in Kansas. As he grows, Clark struggles with his identity and powers. When General Zod, a surviving Kryptonian, arrives on Earth seeking to terraform the planet and rebuild Krypton, Clark must reveal himself as Superman to save humanity. The story explores themes of hope, sacrifice, and what it means to be a hero, culminating in an epic battle for the fate of the world.
